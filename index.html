This site is a showcase of my work for Georgia Tech's CS4641: Machine Learning. This was creted as a visualization tool of the results of our gradient boosting model to predict overall COVID-19 cases given the past 10 days as input. Our model predicts on a county-by-county basis for (almost) the entire United States. To use this tool, simply select a state, select the county of choice, and the data from the results of our model will be plotted. The first 10 days are accurate cumulative numbers from the Johns Hopkins COVID-19 dataset, while the next 7 days are predictions based upon the model.

At the time of writing, this model only predicts for the date range of 12/07/2020 to 12/14/2020, as I do not have the resources or effort to host my model on a 100% uptime, so this site just reads from static data off of our Github. I may, from time to time, reupload more up to date predictions based on my model.
